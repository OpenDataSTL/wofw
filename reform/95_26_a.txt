   REQUEST FOR                                     
    PROPOSALS                                      
        The City of St. Louis is seeking           
 proposals from qualified consultants or           
 consulting firms to conduct an assessment of      
 inmate healthcare needs and develop a Request     
 for Proposals on behalf of the City of St. Louis  
 for comprehensive medical services to inmates     
 confined at the City Justice Center (CJC) and     
 the Medium Security Institution, as well as       
 the intake area of CJC, for detainees of the      
 various law enforcement agencies of the City.     
 The consultant will serve as a technical advisor  
 and liaison to the Dept. of Public Safety and     
 the selected health care provider for a           
 transitional period. For a copy of the RFP,       
 visit stlouis-mo.gov and click on Government,     
 then see all RFPs, RFQs, and RFIs, and then       
 Local Government Services. Proposals due:         
 Friday, Oct. 5, 2012. Closing time: 5 p.m.,       
 CST. Call Eddie Roth at (314) 622-3391 for        
 short procedural inquiries only.
