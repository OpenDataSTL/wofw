PROPOSALS
CITY OF ST. LOUIS
PARKS, RECREATION & FORESTRY

The City of St. Louis is seeking qualified
businesses or organizations to be responsible
for the day-to-day operation, maintenance,
and costs of the West End Community Center
and may charge for office space, gym and
swimming pool usage, and programs offered.

and shall provide all personnel, including
security, if required for an efficient, safe,

the participants.

Department of Parks, 5600 Clayton in Forest
Park, St. Louis, MO 63110, commencing
July 15, 2009.

until 5:00 P.M. on August 17, 2009, at the
Department of Parks.

The City of St. Louis is an Equal
Opportunity Employer, and Respondents
shall comply with Mayor’s Executive Order
#28, as amended.

The City of St. Louis reserves the right
to accept or reject any or all responses or to
cancel this Request For Proposal in part or in
its entirety.
