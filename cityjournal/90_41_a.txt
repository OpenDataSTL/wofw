at the address referenced above.

REQUEST FOR
PROPOSALS

The City of St. Louis is seeking to
contract the services of an experienced Turf
Management Company . The City of St. Louis
Parks Department wants to provide
professional expertise and oversight of skilled
workers performing fine turf maintenance and
ornamental planting maintenance within Forest
Park. The Company must provide an on-site
project manager in Forest Park. This
management will be responsible for the
establishment and maintenance of six (6)
premier turf and ornamental areas within
Forest Park. RFP packets will be available
at the Parks administration building at
5600 Clayton Avenue, St. Louis, MO 63110
from January 11, 2008 to February 11, 2008
from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Bid packages will be opened at 2:00 PM
February 12, 2008 at the Parks administration
Building (5600 Clayton Avenue) and read aloud
at the time.
