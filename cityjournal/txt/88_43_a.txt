Resolution No. 217
WHEREAS, we have been apprised that
on Monday, January 9, 2006, the Central West
End Association will present the 2006 Polk
Award to long time Central West End resident
Arthur (Art) Perry; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Perry is a distinguished
graduate of the St. Louis College of Pharmacy
and currently serves on the College’s Board
of Trustees; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Perry served the St.
Louis community at Kean Drug on Euclid for
more than 27 years; and

WHEREAS,, Mr. Perry has been a
resident of the 4500 block of Westminster for
more than 40 years and has been actively
involved in the growth and development of
the Central West End Neighborhood; and

WHEREAS,, Mr. Perry has served on
the Board of the Central West End Association
for 12 years and as President of the
Westminster Tri-Block Association for 6
terms; and

WHEREAS,, in addition to co-founding
the TWIII Block Builders and the Joint
Community Ministries, Mr. Perry serves as
the President of the 28 th Ward Regular
Democratic Club and as Commissioner of the
22nd Judicial Circuit; and

WHEREAS,, Mr. Perry is an
outstanding member of this community and
we greatly appreciate his many contributions
to the residents of the Central West End
Neighborhood and the citizens of the City of
St. Louis;
NOW THEREFORE BE IT
RESOLVED by the Board of Aldermen of
the City of St. Louis, that we pause in our
deliberations to recognize Arthur (Art) Perry
on the occasion of his receiving the Central
West End Association’s 2006 Polk Award and
we thank him for this many efforts to improve
the quality of life for the residents of this
community and we further direct the Clerk of
this Board to spread a copy of this Resolution
across the minutes of these proceedings and
to prepare a commemorative copy for
presentation to our honoree at a time and place
deemed appropriate by the Sponsor.
Introduced on the 12th day of January, 2006
by:
Honorable Lyda Krewson, Alderwoman 28th Ward
