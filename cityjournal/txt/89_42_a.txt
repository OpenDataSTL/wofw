Resolution No. 177

WHEREAS, we pause in our
deliberations to recognize and honor Twania
Michelle Thomas, a valued and respected
employee of the City of St. Louis who recently
announced her intention to leave city service
to pursue her professional career with BarnesJewish Corporation; and

WHEREAS, Twania was born and
raised in St. Louis and is a distinguished
graduate of Garfield Elementary School,
Normandy Senior High School and Prairie
View A & M University where she received
her B. A. Degree in Architecture in 1999; and

WHEREAS, prior to beginning her
career in public service, Twania worked for
several prestigious local businesses including,
Kwame Building Group, Michael Roth &
Associates and The Vanhooser Partnership;
and

WHEREAS, in March 2001 Twania
joined the Office of the Board of Public Service
for the City of St. Louis and worked closely
with the late Rodney Renke, R.A. and Art
Weissen, R. A. on numerous projects including
the multi-million renovation of the Carnahan
Courthouse; and

WHEREAS, in July 2003 Twania was
assigned the role of Ward Liaison and Capital
Improvement Project Liaison and began the
arduous task of managing more than $25
Million dollars worth of capital improvement
projects in Wards 1, 2, 3, 4, 21, 22 and 27; and

WHEREAS, in addition to her
commitment to the redevelopment of this
community, Twania is admired and respected
by all who know her as a devoted friend and
loving mother to her daughter, Nya Elyse
Dean; and

WHEREAS, Twania is an outstanding
public servant whose hard work,
professionalism, exceptional talents and great
sense of humor will be greatly missed by her
co-workers and the members of this Board of
Aldermen with whom she has so closely
worked;

NOW THEREFORE BE IT
RESOLVED by the Board of Aldermen of
the City of St. Louis, that we break from our
regular agenda to recognize Twania Michelle
Thomas and we thank her, on behalf of the
citizens of the City of St. Louis, for her many
years of dedication to the redevelopment and
renewal of this City and we further direct the
Clerk of this Board to spread a copy of this
Resolution across the minutes of these
proceedings and to prepare a commemorative
copy for presentation to our honoree at a time
and place deemed appropriate by the Sponsor.
