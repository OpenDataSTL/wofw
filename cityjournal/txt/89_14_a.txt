WHEREAS, we have been apprised that

24, 1927 in Otto, Missouri, the son of
Reverend Otto and Christine Bernthal; and

WHEREAS, John attended Roosevelt
High School after his family move to St. Louis

valuable employee at Frisco Railroad and
Concordia Publishing Company from which
he retired in 1990; and

boards and committees;

a copy of this Resolution across the minutes
