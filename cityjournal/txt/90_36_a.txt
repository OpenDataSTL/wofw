COMMUNICATIONS
BOARD ORDER NO. 789

WHEREAS, the Charter of the City of
St Louis grants the Board of Public Service a
number of duties and responsibilities including
regulation, investigation and approval of a
variety of important City transactions; and
WHEREAS, David Visintainer has been
a member of this Board for over 14 years
serving in the administration of three Mayors;
and
WHEREAS, Dave has served the
citizens of St Louis in the capacity of Director
of since 1993, during which the water system
has undergone major infrastructure renewal
and facility upgrades, and,
WHEREAS, Dave’s journeys with the
Water Department has taken him to India, the
Amazon, Japan, Ecuador, Peru and Central
American,
WHEREAS, Dave has excelled under
many challenging circumstances including the
record floods of 1993 on the Missouri and
Mississippi Rivers; and
WHEREAS, Dave has assisted in water
treatment optimization efforts to promote
public health, initially in Central America on
behalf of EPA and, most recently in India and,
WHEREAS, on June 2007 under the
purview of Dave a National Search was
conducted for “BEST TASTING CITY
WATER IN AMERICA” based on taste,
aroma and clarity, the water of St. Louis,
Missouri was chosen number one.
WHEREAS, Dave has distinguished
himself as a member of the Board of Public
Service, by providing technical support,
historical insights and most importantly
sharing his years of experience; and
WHEREAS, Dave has determined to
bring his service to the City to closure by
retiring this November 30, 2007;

NOW, THEREFORE, It is hereby
ordered by this Honorable Board of Public
Service, that Mr. David Visintainer be
recognized for his years of guidance and
direction to the business of this Board and
that a copy of this Board Order become a
permanent record of this public body.
BY ORDER OF THE BOARD OF
