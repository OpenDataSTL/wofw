BOARD OF PUBLIC SERVICES
LETTERS OF INTEREST TO BID
The City of St. Louis is soliciting letters
of interest from companies wishing to bid on
the following construction project at LambertSt. Louis International Airport®.
RECONSTRUCTION OF TAXIWAY V
FROM TAXIWAY F TO RUNWAY 24–
PROJECT 2:
This project involves the Reconstruction
of Taxiway V from a point approximately
1,900 ft. north of Taxiway F to the intersection
of Runway 6/24 (approximately 16,500 SY).
The replacement shall include 18" PCCP and
6" PCCP drainable base on a rubblized salvaged
pavement base. The existing pavement
consists of a 4" bituminous overlay of a 9"
PCCP. The project will also include widening
of the taxiway safety area, replacing lighting
as required, and improving the shoulders. The
DBE requirement for this project will be
provided with the bid documents.
Those firms providing letters of interest
will be provided plans, specifications, and bid
documents by approximately April 7, 2009.
The exact date of bid opening for the project
will be provided in the bid documents, but it
will be approximately April 28, 2009.

Letters of interest should be sent to Bette
Behan, Board of Public Service, 1200 Market
Street, Room 301 City Hall, St. Louis, MO
63013.
