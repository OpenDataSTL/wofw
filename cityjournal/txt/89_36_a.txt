COMMUNICATIONS
Board Bill No. 780

WHEREAS, the Charter of the City of
St Louis grants the Board of Public Service a
number of duties and responsibilities including
regulation, investigation and approval of a
variety of important City transactions; and

WHEREAS, James Suelmann has been
a member of this Board for over 23 years
serving in the administration of four Mayors;
and

WHEREAS, Jim has served the
residents of St Louis as the Director of Public
Utilities (1983-85) and as Street Director
(from 1985 until November 30 2006), during
which time he has faced ice and snow storms,
wind storms and floods, and has provided
support and organization to numerous civic
events such as the World Series, Super Bowl,
Fourth of July Events and a visit by the Pope;
and
WHEREAS, Jim has distinguished
himself as a member of the Board of Public
Service, by providing technical support,
historical insights and most importantly
sharing his years of experience; and
WHEREAS, Jim has determined to
bring his service to the City to closure by
retiring this November 30, 2006; and
NOW, THEREFORE, It is hereby
ordered by this Honorable Board of Public
Service, that Mr. James Suelmann be
recognized for his years of guidance and
direction to the business of this Board and
that a copy of this Board Order become a
permanent record of this public body.
BY ORDER OF THE BOARD OF
PUBLIC SERVICE, NOVEMBER 28, 2006
