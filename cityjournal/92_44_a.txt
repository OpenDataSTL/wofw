Statutes of Missouri, 2000, as amended (the

inclusive, as amended); containing a
description of the boundaries of the Area in

the people of the City; approving the Plan
attached hereto and incorporated herein as
Attachment “B”, pursuant to Section 99.430;
finding that there is a feasible financial plan
for the development of the Area which affords

maximum opportunity for development of the
Area by private enterprise; finding that no
property in the Area may be acquired by the
Land Clearance for Redevelopment Authority
(“LCRA”) of the City of St. Louis, a public
body corporate and politic created under
Missouri law, through the exercise of eminent
domain; finding that the property within the
Area is unoccupied, but if it should become
occupied, the Redeveloper shall be responsible
for relocating any eligible occupants displaced
as a result of implementation of the Plan;
finding that financial aid may be necessary to
enable the Area to be redeveloped in
accordance with the Plan; finding that there
shall be available up to eight (8) year real estate
tax abatement; and pledging cooperation of
this St. Louis Board of Aldermen (“Board”)
and requesting various officials, departments,
boards and agencies of the City to cooperate

manner consistent with the Plan.

Resolution No. 262

Michael David Walker
WHEREAS, Michael David Walker was
the second of four children born on January 6,
1964, in Bronx, New York to the union of Ida
Mae (Hagler) and David Randolph Walker.
His father preceded him in death.; and

WHEREAS, Michael grew up in St.
Louis after spending early childhood years in
Boston, Massachusetts. He received his
elementary and high school education at
Parochial Schools in St. Louis City; and
WHEREAS, Michael accepted Christ at
a young age and was baptized at Calvary
Missionary Baptist Church. In recent years,
he returned to Calvary M.B. Church
remembering his past participation in plays,
fashion shows and events under the guidance
of his grandmother, Retha McNary Eubanks;
and

WHEREAS, Michael had an
adventurous spirit and decided to enlist in the
United States Marines. He was stationed in
California where he met, fell in love with and
married Shirley (Boyd). To this union was
born two daughters, April Lynn Walker and
Cherie Walker; and
WHEREAS, upon his return to St.
Louis, Michael met Jill Hill and a son, Jared
Hill was born; and
WHEREAS, Michael was given many
talents by God, all with an incredible gift to
take pen in hand and create beautiful art work
regardless of the medium used. His creative
spirit led him also into the culinary field. He
loved to cook; and
WHEREAS, Michael had a dream to
open his own food establishment. Until that
dream became a reality, he started feeding the
homeless. He, along with his mother prepared
food on Wednesdays and took it to places
where those less fortunate gathered. Michael
was proud of his traveling “food wagon.”
Sometimes when he was able to, he would
give socks and gloves; and
WHEREAS, Michael loved his family.
He will be missed by his son, Jared Hill;
daughters April Walker and Cherie Walker;
mother, Ida Ford and devoted stepfather,
Louis Ford; grandmother, Retha McNary
Eubanks; three brothers, Candies Walker,
David Walker and Anthony Walker; two step
brothers, Mark Jones (Deborah) and Anthony
Jones (deceased); three sisters, Retha Jackson
(Tracy), April Griffin (Wil) and Keeva Cole,
and a host of aunts, uncles, nieces, nephews,
cousins and friends that will miss him and his
zest for life; and
WHEREAS, Michael was truly a unique
and beautiful spirit living his life on his own
terms.
NOW THEREFORE BE IT
RESOLVED by the Board of Aldermen of
the City of St. Louis that we pause to
remember the many contributions of Michael
David Walker to the citizens of the City of St.
Louis and we join with his many friends in
expressing our sorrow at his passing, and we
further direct the Clerk of this Board to spread
a copy of this Resolution across the minutes
of these proceedings and to prepare a memorial
copy for presentation to the Walker family, at
a time and place deemed appropriate by the
Sponsor.
