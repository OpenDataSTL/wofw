HEARINGS
Advance Auto Parts, 6216 Morganford,
Conditional Use Permit No. 102480, to show
cause why permit should not be revoked
effective March 16, 2005, due to the Boards
findings that the imposed conditions of the
conditional use permit have been violated
pursuant to Section 26.100.030 of the Revised
Code of the City (Ordinance 64051),
interested parties appeared, were sworn in
and heard. The parts manager for the store
appeared and explained that the store is taking
steps to comply with the conditions. He
agreed that the conditions, especially
concerning the hours on Sunday and repairs
being performed on the lot, were violated. The
Board, after hearing from the parts manager,
voted to uphold its decision of March 1, 2005
that the conditions were being violated, that
there was sufficient evidence to revoke the
conditional use permit, but it would not revoke
the conditional use permit at this time, but it
would revoke the conditional use permit if
any of the conditions issued on October 7,
1997 were violated in the future.
